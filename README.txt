== Description ==

[VaocherApp](https://www.vaocherapp.com/) is a simple, fully-automated and powerful gift voucher online system that can help you to set up and start selling your own gift cards, gift vouchers and gift certificates in your WordPress website with ease. If you use WooCommerce plugin in your WordPress website, your customers can redeem their gift vouchers directly in their shopping cart.

The setup and installation process is simple, we have automated most of the steps, you just need to sign up a new VaocherApp account, connect to payment provider of your choice, place the VaocherApp Checkout form into your website and that's it. You can sell gift vouchers at any time, 24/7.

Once an order is placed, VaocherApp will handle everything for you, from processing their payment to delivery of the digital gift voucher design. The process is fully automated and you get paid into your bank account almost immediately.

Features:

- beautiful gift voucher design with your branding
- fully automated: your customers place orders and VaocherApp will send them the vouchers via email
- easy to install checkout form using our shortcode [vaocherapp]
- powerful dashboard for you and your team to manage the sold vouchers (e.g. redeem, issue complementary vouchers...)
- design your own gift voucher or choose from one of our designs
- specify expiry dates of your choice (e.g. 12 months after purchase date, or fixed date, or never expires)
- marketing integrations (e.g. Google Analytics, Mailchimp, Campaign Monitor...)


== Installation ==

Install VaocherApp plugin via the [WordPress plugin store](https://wordpress.org/plugins/vaocher-app/).

= Activation =
1. After installed and activated the VaocherApp plugin, go to Settings -> VaocherApp page
2. Follow the instruction on that page to connect your VaocherApp account with your WordPress website
4. Add the shortcode `[vaocherapp]` to a page where you want your customers to purchase the gift vouchers
5. Enjoy gift voucher sales!


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

We have a [Help Center](https://www.vaocherapp.com/docs) where you can quickly find your answers quickly.

= What is VaocherApp fee? =

There is no monthly or setup fees. We charge a fee of 3% when you sold a gift voucher (you can opt for your customers to pay this if you prefer).

If you are selling more and don't want to pay the 3% fee, upgrade to a paid plan to enjoy selling with 0% VaocherApp fee.

Visit [www.vaocherapp.com/pricing](https://www.vaocherapp.com/pricing) for more info.

= How and when do I get paid? =

You get the gift voucher revenue (less any payment processing fees and our fee) almost immediately.
We do not hold on to any funds. You can get the money into your designated bank account in 1-2 working days.

= What can I sell? =

You can sell anything you like! From simple gift cards/vouchers to prepaid services...

= What about WooCommerce support? =

WooCommerce plugin is supported. Once enabled, your customers can redeem gift vouchers in your WooCommerce store.
